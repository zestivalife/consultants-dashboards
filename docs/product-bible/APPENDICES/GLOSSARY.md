## Terms

### Zestiva One Platform

The unified enterprise platform for all Zestiva products.

### Product

A commercial or operational offering such as Nuetra, FitEatsy, CRM, LIMS or AI Platform.

### Capability

A reusable business function consumed by one or more products.

### Product Registry

The system that records each product's modules, routes, permissions, widgets, dashboards, workflows and requirements.

### Capability Registry

The catalog of reusable platform capabilities.

### Tenant

An organization or customer boundary used for data isolation and access scoping.

### Workspace

An operational unit inside an organization, such as department, branch, clinic, region or project.

### Effective Access

The resolved access outcome after evaluating platform, organization, workspace, permission template, overrides, feature flags, subscription and business rules.

### RBAC

Role-Based Access Control. Defines authority through roles and permissions.

### ABAC

Attribute-Based Access Control. Defines authority through contextual attributes such as organization, workspace, ownership, subscription or assignment.

### Provisioning

The process of converting an invitation or user creation request into identity, organization, workspace, permission, feature and dashboard assignments.

### Workflow Engine

A reusable engine that executes stateful business processes.

### Dashboard Engine

The system that generates dashboards dynamically from effective access context.

### Navigation Engine

The system that generates menus, routes, quick actions and visibility from effective access context.

### Audit by Default

The rule that every critical business action must create an immutable audit event.

### Product Bible

The enterprise source of truth for business, product, architecture, UX, engineering, security, operations, AI and governance decisions.
